Awards and Recognition

Chemference 23

The research work on environmental remediation, focusing on repurposing waste EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) into adhesive, was presented at the national chemical engineering conference, Chemference 23.

Undergraduate Research Showcase at IIT Gandhinagar

It also secured the 2nd Best Poster Prize at the Undergraduate Research Showcase held at IIT Gandhinagar, where 100 undergraduate students presented their research.

Boeing BUILD 3.0

Moreover, Jinious Sheth recently participated in Boeing's prestigious program named Boeing BUILD 3.0, where 1200 startups showcased their innovative ideas. Making it to the regional finals, Jinious emerged as one of the top 4 finalists in fierce competition and was further among the top 28 startups nationwide.

Featured in Gujarati Newspaper

The innovative research on converting waste EPS into a biodegradable adhesive was recently featured in a leading Gujarati newspaper.
